GRIET

COLLECTION: The present collection of library is 84000 books with over 10000 titles 300 back volumes of research journals and 600 project reports. It is receiving 250+ research journals National and International. It also has collection of over 500 CD ROMS and Audio-visual lessons.

SERVICES: The Library is providing the following services to the readers.

1 REFERENCE SERVICE: Providing information about and from the sources available in library at appropriate time to the users when they come with queries.

2 DOCUMENT DELIVERY SERVICE: The document available in the library stacks will be issued to the users during office hours for a fixed duration of days.

3 REPROGRAPHIC SERVICE: Xerox facility available to the library users throughout the library opening hours at minimum charges.

4 CURRENT AWARENESS SERVICE: As part of current awareness service the library issues current periodicals, display of new arrivals, notifying the forth-coming conferences / seminars etc., and important notifications

5 DATABASE SEARCH SERVICE: At present the literature search is available through INTERNET and ONLINE ACCESS to IEEE database (limited) and ACM journals and periodicals

6 OPAC: An Online Public Access Catalogue terminal is kept to the users to facilitate them to search through catalogue to ascertain the availability of documents (presently limited to books and audio-visual materials only). It can be searched using various options like title, author, subject, publisher etc. in a user friendly way.

7 FUTURE PLANS: Library is initiating steps to become digital and planning to go online. It is in the process of implementing web-enabled user friendly library software. A new library building is coming up with a carpet area of 310000 sft.
